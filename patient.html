Flow Chart

Algorithm

1. Fill up the online application form if you are a new patient if its not don't fill up and proceed to the number 2
2. After you finish filling up the form go to the Clinic
3. Go to the patient desk and give your ID to check your data in the database
4. Wait for your turn in to go in the doctors office.
5. Upon your name called go to the doctors office.
6. The doctor will diagnose your health condition.
7. After diagnosing your health condition the doctor will tell your health condition and will give you a prescription.
9. End
